  • 1984 - In 1984 Arsene decided to go back into professional football but this time as a football manager. The first football team managed by Arsene Wenger was Nancy.


  • 1987 - In 1987 Nancy got relegated and Arsene had the permission to break out of his contract and move to Monaco. He managed monaco for seven years before he was sacked from his job.

  • 1997 - Arsenal started getting rewards of the risk they took with appointing Arsene Wenger.


  • 1998 - He improved the clubs playing style and also led the club to a Premier League title and Fa cup double at the end of his first full season. He was also the first foreign manager to achieve this feet.


  • 2003 - Arsene's greatest achievement came in the year 2003-2004 where he set up a team that went the whole premier league season unbeaten. A feet still yet to be achieved by any other premier league team.


  • 2006 - From the year 2006 things became difficult for Arsene at arsenal. They left highbury stadium and moved to nearby Emirates stadium.   • 2017 - This came the angry years where the fans refused to accept how the club was being managed. The fans wanted trophies and success but unfortunately Arsene wasn't able to offer that.


  • 2018 - Arsene was relieved of his duty but till date he still remains Arsenal's greatest manager after spending 24 years managing the greatest football club in London.




Arsene Wenger's achievement at Arsenal in unmatchable and till today he remains the longest serving manager in the history of the Premier league. Only Sir Alex Ferguson of Manchester United comes close.
